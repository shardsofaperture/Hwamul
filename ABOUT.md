# About Hwamul Logistics Planner

Hwamul Logistics Planner is a local-first operations app designed for planning teams that need a practical way to maintain supplier data, apply packaging and lead-time logic, estimate transportation outcomes, and track customs attributes.

## Design goals
- **Simple local deployment** using Streamlit + SQLite.
- **Editable operational master data** through in-app admin grids.
- **Transparent planning outputs** that can be exported as CSV.
- **Audit-friendly customs tracking** with effective-dated HTS/tariff records.

## Who this is for
- Logistics planners
- Sourcing/operations analysts
- Trade compliance collaborators
- Teams that need quick what-if planning without a heavy platform rollout
